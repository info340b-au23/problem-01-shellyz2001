Wang Miao

Wang Miao, the protagonist in Cixin Liu's novel The Three-Body Problem, is an eminent scientist specializing in nanotechnology. He was unwittingly drawn into a world of alien encounters and impending peril for humanity when he discovered that the unpredictable temperature and the sun's erratic behavior in the virtual reality game "Three-Body" mirror a real existence. As secrets unravel, Wang Miao undergoes profound transformations, immersing himself in the depths of existential contemplation and embarking on an exploration of the universe's mysteries.

Reasons I Admire Wang Miao:

  1. Emotional Stability: He remains calm and composed when facing severe challenges
  2. Courage: He shows great courage during dangerous and unpredictable situations
  3. Resilience: He refuses to give in to despair even when confronted with psychological pressure
